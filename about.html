About Gandy

Gandalf Ebrecht is a classical guitarist based in Grand Junction, where he studies Music Performance at Colorado Mesa University. A dedicated and nontraditional student, he brings a focused intensity and lived experience to his musicianship, balancing academic excellence with a strong independent work ethic. His approach to classical guitar emphasizes clarity of tone, expressive phrasing, and thoughtful interpretation, drawing from both traditional repertoire and his broader musical background in punk and metal. This cross-genre sensibility informs a playing style that is both disciplined and emotionally direct. Ebrecht has performed in a variety of settings, including intimate recitals and live theater productions, where his playing adds depth and atmosphere to dramatic works. Whether on stage or in practice, he is committed to refining his craft and communicating the expressive potential of the classical guitar with honesty and conviction.
